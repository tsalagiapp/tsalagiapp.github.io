Quick troubleshooting

  • Restart the app (swipe up from the app switcher) and try again.
  • Ensure you have the Cherokee keyboard enabled (Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Add New Keyboard… → Cherokee).
  • Sound issues? Check your device volume and silent switch; try headphones.
  • Reinstall the app if problems persist (this may reset local progress).

FAQ

How do I report a bug?
